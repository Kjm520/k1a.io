Buy Later - 30 Day Countdown

About the App

Buy Later helps prevent impulse purchases in today's modern online shopping environment.

Have you ever spontaneously bought something and then 3 days later realized you did not actually want or need it?

It can be easy to open an app and buy something in a minute or less. Buy Later is designed to create a buffer in that minute. The app provides a minimal, clean interface where you can list things you want as you think of them, and then once they make it through the fixed 30 day hold period, can be bought (if you still think its a good idea).

Features

  • Minimalist user interface
  • Fixed 30 day hold period
  • Notifications for an item's completion (optional)
  • Always Free: 100% ad-free, no sign-in, no subscriptions
  • Private: All data lives on your device for maximum privacy. No data is retained for any purpose.

System Requirements

  • iOS 14.0 or later
  • iPhone and iPad Optimized
  • Offline-First: All data stays on your device

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new item?
- Tap the + button on the Pending tab, enter your item, and hit Save. Your 30-day timer starts immediately and automatically.
How do I delete an item?
- Swipe left on the item that you want to delete to reveal the delete button.
Where can I see completed items?
- Switch to the Completed tab to review all items whose countdown has finished.
Can I customize the timer length?
- Buy Later uses a fixed 30-day period deliberately. This creates a standard procedure that the app was designed around. It cannot be changed.
Where is my data stored
- All data is stored locally on your device. It is not accessible anywhere except on the device where it was created. If you delete the app, all of the data will be lost. You can view our privacy policy here.
