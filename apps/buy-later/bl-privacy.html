Privacy Policy

Buy Later is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data—including your items, countdown timers, and completion status—is stored locally on your device and never leaves the app.

Data Storage

All information is saved in secure on-device storage on your iPhone or iPad. We do not synchronize or back up your data to any external servers unless you choose to do so via iCloud or other device-managed backups.

Third-Party Services

Buy Later does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app functions entirely offline, ensuring no external data collection or network dependency.
