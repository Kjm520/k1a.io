Buy Later

Minimalist 30-Day Countdown

About the App

Buy Later helps you curb impulse purchases and manage any 30-day countdown with a simple, distraction-free interface. Add items quickly, track when they become due, and review completed tasks—all without ads or subscriptions.

System Requirements

  • iOS 14.0 or later
  • iPhone and iPad Optimized
  • Offline-First: All data stays on your device

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new item?
Tap the + button on the Pending tab, enter your item, and hit Save. Your 30-day timer starts immediately.
Where can I see completed items?
Switch to the Completed tab to review all items whose countdown has finished.
Can I customize the timer length?
Currently Buy Later uses a fixed 30-day period. Future updates may include custom durations—stay tuned!
Is my data private?
Yes. All data is stored locally on your device. We don’t collect or share any personal information.
