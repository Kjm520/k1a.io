Buy Later

Minimalist 30-Day Countdown

About the App

Buy Later helps you curb impulse purchases and manage any 30-day countdown with a simple, distraction-free interface. Add items quickly, track when they become due, and review completed tasks—all without ads or subscriptions.

Each item gets a 30-day “cool-off” period. If you still want it after 30 days, you’re in the clear. You can also use Buy Later for any 30-day countdown—whether it’s a to-do list, habit tracker, or project deadline.

Features

  • Minimalist user interface
  • 30-Day Cooldown: Items automatically move from Pending to Completed once the timer ends
  • Quick Add: Tap “+” and type in under a second—no extra screens or settings
  • See Completed: Celebrate or clear your wins in a dedicated tab
  • Always Free: 100% ad-free, no sign-in, no subscriptions
  • Offline-Only: All data lives on your device for maximum privacy

Whether you’re budgeting, saving, or simply want to think twice before you buy, Buy Later gives you the space to make smarter spending decisions.

System Requirements

  • iOS 14.0 or later
  • iPhone and iPad Optimized
  • Offline-First: All data stays on your device

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new item?
Tap the + button on the Pending tab, enter your item, and hit Save. Your 30-day timer starts immediately.
Where can I see completed items?
Switch to the Completed tab to review all items whose countdown has finished.
Can I customize the timer length?
Currently Buy Later uses a fixed 30-day period deliberately. This creates a standard procedure that the app was designed around.
Is my data private?
Yes. All data is stored locally on your device. We don’t collect or share any personal information. You can view our privacy policy here.
